Understand the app

📱 About the app

OX MONTH is

an app to check daily follow-through simply—without heavy logging—and manage habits and goals for the month.

OX MONTH is designed for anyone who wants lightweight, honest daily check-ins. It currently supports nine languages—including Korean, English, Japanese, Spanish, Portuguese (Brazil), French, German, Hindi, and Indonesian—and we are gradually rolling out to 41 countries as listings and localization progress.

Understanding the app

📱 About the app

Every recurring bill in one place

SubPing helps you manage not only streaming-style subscriptions but also phone bills, insurance, rent, tuition, and every other recurring expense in one place.

It automatically organizes repeating charges and shows this month’s total and what’s due next—at a glance.

Spot inactive subscriptions and unnecessary spending easily, so saving money feels natural.

SubPing isn’t just a tracker—it’s built to change how money flows for you.
