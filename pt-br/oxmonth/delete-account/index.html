Exclusão de conta

App: 0 · Desenvolvedor: 1

You can delete your account directly in the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Account
  4. Tap Delete account

When you delete

  • All user data is deleted immediately.
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered.
